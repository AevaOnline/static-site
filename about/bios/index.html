Bios and Headshots

1 minute read

Aeva Black is a non-binary hacker, public speaker, and open source software developer, who currently serves as the Section Chief for Open Source Software Security at the U.S. Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency (CISA). They previously held roles at several non-profits -- including the Open Source Initiative, the Open Source Security Foundation, and the OpenStack Foundation -- and led development teams at a few companies -- including Microsoft, Hewlett Packard Enterprise, and IBM.

Aeva spends their spare time tending a small garden, riding motorcycles, and supporting local LGBTQ+ community in Seattle.
