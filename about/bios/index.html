Slightly Longer (1,000 characters)

Aeva Black is an incurably queer geek and a dot-com veteran, passionate about privacy, ethics, and inclusivity. They work in Azure's Office of the CTO and serve as a member of the Board of the Open Source Initiative, a member of the OpenSSF Technical Advisory Council, and a Board Shadow in the Cloud Native Computing Foundation. Aeva previously served on the OpenStack Technical Committee and on the Board of the Consent Academy. Many years ago, Aeva founded the OpenStack Ironic project, wrote a lot of Python, and managed a few small MySQL databases.

Having grown up a gender-non-conforming third-culture-kid, Aeva's unique perspective and precise communication style often inspires others to question their assumptions. Their recent writing includes contributing to "Transending: An Anthology Of Trans Buddhist Voices" (2019), and being the technical editor for "Trust In Computer Systems And The Cloud" (2021).
