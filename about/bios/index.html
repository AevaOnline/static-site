Bios and Headshots

1 minute read

Aeva Black is an open source hacker, advocate, and international public speaker with 25 years of experience building digital infrastructure and leading open source projects at successful technology companies. They are the Section Chief for Open Source Software Security at CISA, and serve as the Secretary of the Board of the Open Source Initiative. Aeva spends their spare time riding motorcycles and supporting local LGBTQ+ community.
