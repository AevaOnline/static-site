A little about me ...

I'm incurably geeky, an ethical hacker, and a chronically poor sleeper. I am politically queer because there isn't any other ethical choice under capitalism. I practice a mixture of the buddha dharma and a combination of hindu, jewish, and wiccan traditions I grew up with. I believe in the oxford comma. I'm also a survivor of a long-term disability (I spent half of my 20's home-bound with lyme disease) and transgender.

Yes, I talk openly about being trans, both on here and on other social media. Publicly transitioning is a complicated and personal aspect of each person's story. When I transitioned mid-career, it left me jobless and uninsured for about a year during the Trump administration. This taught me a lot more about privilege. I'm thankful for the chance to reflect on and re-contextualize my life, and grateful to all the friends who supported me.
