Bios

1 minute read

Short

Aeva Black is an incurably queer geek, a Seattle'ite, and an open source hacker in the Azure Office of the CTO, passionate about making our digital world more secure, ethical, and inclusive. At the moment, their efforts are focused on open source software supply chain security, cloud native computing, and confidential computing. In a past life, they led the creation of OpenStack's Bare Metal Cloud project, lived on a tiny farm in the Olympic Mountains, and raised a pair of wolves.
